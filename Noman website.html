About Me

Biography

I am a dedicated Mechanical Engineering Researcher specializing in Metal Additive Manufacturing and Biomechanics. Currently pursuing my MSc by Research at Universiti Malaysia Pahang Al-Sultan Abdullah, I focus on developing innovative solutions for biomedical implant design through advanced manufacturing techniques.

My research combines cutting-edge technologies like topology optimization, finite element analysis, and machine learning to create next-generation medical devices. With a strong background in both academic research and industrial applications, I bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation.
